NieR:Automata is an action role playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. From the great mastermind of the NieR series, Yoko Taro; NieR:Automata sees the story of androids fighting off a machine-alien race to take back our planet! Follow 2B, 9S, and A2, as they fight back the machines and discover what it means to be human.
