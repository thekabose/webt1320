NieR:Automata

Characters

2B

2B is NieR:Automata's main character. She is a battle type android that the player will control for majority of the game. Equipped with a large variety of weapons and able to hold two at a time along with her pod, she is a fearsome fighter and cold character.

9S

9S is the game's second main character. He is a scanner type android that specializes at hacking and gathering information. He is assigned to assist 2B in her mission by giving hacking support and light combat assistance.

A2

A2 is the third and final main character. A rogue attacker type android deemed extremely dangerous, command has ordered 2B and 9S to either eliminate or avoid her. Her reasons for going rogue unknown, perhaps she learned a secret command did not want her to know?
